Why SUBHA ELAN Chandapura?

The project is a 1.26-acre integrated lifestyle development, with well-designed residential apartments. SUBHA ELAN offers a combination of 1BHK, 2BHK and 3BHK apartments having 132 flats with world class amenities. Designed and executed to superior standards, with abundant lifestyle amenities, Subha Elan is yet another of our superb project that brings home the true value of good living with comfort and luxury.

Be well connected with neighbours with our App-based community management system, set up for your convenience. Come, enjoy a lifetime of joy in an elegant environment and enhanced quality of life that you have always dreamed of.

Flourishing Commercial Hub

  1. SUBHA ELAN is located near Chandapura Circle, which employs close to 8 lakh individuals across top companies like Wipro, Infosys, TCS, Motorola, Intel, Tata Power and Robert Bosch.
  2. Jigini & Bommasandra Industrial Area which employees close to 1 lakh people in companies such as Arvind Mills, Biocon, Mahindra Reva, Tata Advanced Materials, Carl Zeiss & HCL are a mere 10 minute drive.
  3. Hosur Road itself is a huge manufacturing hub, as well as medium & small scale industrial area of Bangalore employing more than 5 Lakh individuals.

Most Sought-After, Yet Economical

  1. Prevalent prices in Electronic City pivot for homes from Grade “A” developers charging around INR 5,000 to 5,750 SQ FT, with most inventories available around INR 4,000 SQ FT.
  2. Huge price in Electronic City corridor combined with large apartment sizes make most of the residential projects in this location un-affable to many.
  3. SUBHA ELAN offers admirable living amenities, intelligently sized apartments and the apartments are priced appealingly, making it a fine investment opportunity for early investors.
